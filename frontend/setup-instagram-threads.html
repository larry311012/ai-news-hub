Before You Start - Make Sure You Have

  • An Instagram Business or Creator account (personal accounts won't work)
  • A Facebook Page linked to your Instagram account
  • Facebook/Meta account credentials to authorize
  • (Optional) Threads account for text-based posting

Meta (Facebook) owns both Instagram and Threads. With one OAuth connection, you can publish to both platforms simultaneously or individually.

Instagram

  • Captions (up to 2,200 characters)

Threads

One Connection, Two Platforms

How to check your account type:

  1. Open Instagram app and go to your profile
  2. Tap the menu (three lines) → Settings
  3. Look for "Account type" or "Switch to Professional Account"
  4. If you see "Business" or "Creator" - you're ready!
  5. If you see "Personal" - you need to switch to Professional
How to convert to Business/Creator account
  1. Open Instagram → Profile → Menu → Settings
  2. Tap "Switch to Professional Account"
  3. Choose "Business" or "Creator" (both work)
  4. Select a category for your account
  5. Add contact information (email/phone)
  6. Connect to a Facebook Page (create one if needed)
  7. Done! Your account is now ready for API access

Note: Converting is free and reversible. You can always switch back to Personal later.

Click the button below to connect your Instagram Business account through Facebook OAuth. A popup window will open for authorization.

What to expect:

  1. A Facebook/Meta login popup will open
  2. Log in with your Facebook account
  3. Select the Facebook Page linked to Instagram
  4. Review and approve the requested permissions
  5. You'll be redirected back automatically

Permissions we'll request:

  • instagram_basic - Access your Instagram account info
  • instagram_content_publish - Create posts on your behalf
  • pages_show_list - View your Facebook Pages
Troubleshooting: Popup blocked or not working

If the popup is blocked:

  • Check your browser's popup blocker settings
  • Allow popups for this website
  • Try again after allowing popups

If you can't see your Instagram account:

  • Make sure you've converted to Business/Creator account
  • Verify your Instagram is linked to a Facebook Page
  • Check you're logged into the correct Facebook account
